🪖 INFANTRY DRAFT — d20 CHART

Declare draft → costs 1 turn → General rolls d20 → use +/− in inventory to set result. Monarchy & Oligarchy cannot draft. Infantry burn after defending one turn.
🎲 1 = FAIL — turn lost, 0 infantry
🎲 2–4 = 1 infantry
🎲 5–8 = 2 infantry
🎲 9–13 = 3 infantry  (most likely)
🎲 14–17 = 4 infantry
🎲 18–19 = 5 infantry
🎲 20 = 6 infantry + FREE TURN 🎉
